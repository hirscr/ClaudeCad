# Mike Wazowski (8cm tall)

Create Mike Wazowski from Monsters Inc as a multi-colored 3D model using Compound (not BuildPart).

## Coordinate System
- Z = up/down, Y = forward/back, X = left/right
- Eye looks toward +Y
- Units: millimeters

---

## 1) Body (large green sphere)
- Sphere: diameter 60mm
- Center at (0, 0, 30)
- Color: green

---

## 2) Eye white
- Sphere: diameter 30mm
- Center at (0, 25, 38)
- Color: white

---

## 3) Iris
- Sphere: diameter 16mm
- Center at (0, 38, 38)
- Color: teal

---

## 4) Pupil
- Sphere: diameter 8mm
- Center at (0, 43, 38)
- Color: black

---

## 5) Eyelid
- Hemisphere: radius 16mm, flat side down
- Center at (0, 30, 48)
- Color: green

---

## 6) Horns
- Two cones: base radius 4mm, tip radius 1mm, height 12mm, pointing up
- Left at (-12, -5, 58)
- Right at (12, -5, 58)
- Color: green

---

## 7) Mouth
- Box: 25mm wide × 8mm deep × 6mm tall
- Center at (0, 28, 18)
- Color: dark red

---

## 8) Arms
- Two cylinders: diameter 6mm, length 25mm, pointing outward
- Left arm at (-32, 0, 30)
- Right arm at (32, 0, 30)
- Color: green

---

## 9) Hands
- Two spheres: diameter 10mm
- Left hand at (-45, 0, 30)
- Right hand at (45, 0, 30)
- Color: green

---

## 10) Legs
- Two cylinders: diameter 7mm, length 20mm, pointing down
- Left leg at (-12, 0, -5)
- Right leg at (12, 0, -5)
- Color: green

---

## 11) Feet
- Two boxes: 12mm wide × 18mm deep × 5mm tall
- Left foot at (-12, 3, -15)
- Right foot at (12, 3, -15)
- Color: green

---

## Colors Summary
- Body, eyelid, horns, arms, hands, legs, feet: green
- Eye white: white
- Iris: teal
- Pupil: black
- Mouth: dark red
