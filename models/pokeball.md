# Pokeball (5cm diameter)

Create a Pokeball as a multi-colored 3D model using Compound (not BuildPart).

## Coordinate System
- Z = up/down, Y = forward/back, X = left/right
- Button faces toward +Y
- Units: millimeters

---

## 1) Top half (red hemisphere)
- Hemisphere: radius 25mm, flat side down, dome up
- Center at (0, 0, 25)
- Color: red

---

## 2) Bottom half (white hemisphere)
- Hemisphere: radius 25mm, flat side up, dome down
- Center at (0, 0, 25)
- Color: white

---

## 3) Center band (black ring)
- Ring: outer radius 26mm, inner radius 24mm, height 6mm
- Center at (0, 0, 22)
- Color: black

---

## 4) Button base (white circle)
- Cylinder: diameter 14mm, height 3mm, pointing forward
- Center at (0, 26, 25)
- Color: white

---

## 5) Button center (gray)
- Cylinder: diameter 8mm, height 4mm, pointing forward
- Center at (0, 27, 25)
- Color: dark gray

---

## 6) Button ring (black outline)
- Ring: outer diameter 16mm, inner diameter 12mm, height 2mm, pointing forward
- Center at (0, 26.5, 25)
- Color: black

---

## Colors Summary
- Top: red
- Bottom: white
- Band, button ring: black
- Button base: white
- Button center: dark gray
