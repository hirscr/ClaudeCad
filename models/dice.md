## D6 (six-sided) - EASIEST
- Cube with rounded edges (fillets)
- Dots as spherical indents (subtract spheres)
- Classic pip layout: 1-6 dots per face
- No text needed - this one is doable!

## D4 (four-sided tetrahedron)
- Tetrahedron shape
- Numbers 1-4 engraved on faces
- **Challenge:** Text/numbers not supported in Build123d

## D8 (eight-sided octahedron)
- Octahedron shape (two pyramids base-to-base)
- Numbers 1-8 engraved
- **Challenge:** Text not supported

## D10 (ten-sided)
- Pentagonal trapezohedron
- Numbers 0-9 engraved
- **Challenge:** Complex geometry + text

## D12 (twelve-sided dodecahedron)
- Dodecahedron (12 pentagonal faces)
- Numbers 1-12 engraved
- **Challenge:** Complex geometry + text

## D20 (twenty-sided icosahedron)
- Icosahedron (20 triangular faces)
- Numbers 1-20 engraved
- **Challenge:** Complex geometry + text

---

## Limitation Note
Build123d doesn't support text/font engraving easily.

**Workarounds:**
- D6: Use dots (spherical indents) instead of numbers - classic style
- Others: Could use simple geometric symbols instead of numbers?
- Or accept blank faces and engrave numbers post-print?

**Recommendation:** Start with D6 (dots) - it's fully achievable.
